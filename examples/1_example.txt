Classic Lasagna Bolognese Recipe:
Ingredients:
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 carrot, finely chopped
- 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
- 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- 1 cup beef broth
- 1/2 cup red wine
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 12 lasagna noodles, cooked
- 2 cups ricotta cheese
- 1 egg
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:
1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook until vegetables are tender.
2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, and red wine. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Simmer for about 1 hour.
3. In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, basil, and parsley.
4. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F (190Â°C). 
5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce, followed by a layer of cooked lasagna noodles, then a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture, and a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers.
6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
7. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your delicious Lasagna Bolognese!
