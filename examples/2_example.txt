To prepare "Arroz con Camarones y Panela," you will need the following ingredients:

- 1 cup of white rice
- 1 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 small red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 small green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 small carrot, diced
- 1 cup of crushed tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons of panela (or dark brown sugar)
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 cups of fish or vegetable broth
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Cilantro for garnish

Instructions:
1. In a large skillet or pan, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, red and green bell peppers, and carrot. Cook until vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.
2. Add the rice to the skillet and cook for another 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, fish or vegetable broth, panela, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
5. While the rice is cooking, season the shrimp with salt and pepper.
6. In a separate pan, heat a little oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and cooked through.
7. Once the rice is done, gently fold in the cooked shrimp.
8. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
9. Serve hot, garnished with freshly chopped cilantro.

Enjoy your flavorful "Arroz con Camarones y Panela"!
